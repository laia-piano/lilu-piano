Exam and Competition Video Recording

As the examination institutions of colleges such as ABRSM began to provide online exam, more and more teachers and students began to choose to take online exams by recording videos.

In Opulent Pianoforte piano showroom, we provide not only the best pianos such as Steinway and Blüthner, but also the professional recording equipment such as RODE M5 condenser microphone and YAMAHA AG06 Mixer.

Whether you are taking entry level exams(ABRSM G1 to G8), or advanced diploma exams like (ATCL, LTCL, or DipABRSM), Opulent Pianoforte will provide you with the highest quality recordings to achieve higher mark.
