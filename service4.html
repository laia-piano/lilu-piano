Professional Piano Purchase Consultation

There are currently hundreds of piano brands in the world, but there are very few piano brands that truly have high-quality manufacturing standards.

In 15 years of operation, Opulent Pianoforte has accumulated sales experience of thousands of top high-end pianos from all over the world (including more than 300 Steinway and other top European brands). From 19th century to today, we have all kinds of information and knowledge of all major brands of pianos in the world, whether it is a new piano or a used/rebuilt piano.

We will explain the most authentic core information of each brand of piano to customers, so that customers will fully understand the true value of each brand of piano in the international market and completely avoid unnecessary costs caused by false concepts created by merchants. Finally, we will provide the most cost-effective purchasing suggestions according to the needs and budget of customers.
