YAMAHA HQ series is the automatic performance version of YAMAHA upright piano classic U series in Japan during 1994-1997. HQ300B is equivalent to U300(same as the U3 on sale today) that is equipped with a high-precision automatic performance system.

YAMAHA invited pianists to play on the piano equipped with the automatic performance system. The system would read the details of each key touch of the playing and generate special files as a record. You can accurately reproduce the pianist's live performance on any YAMAHA piano equipped with the YAMAHA automatic performance system, giving you an immersive experience of live performance.
